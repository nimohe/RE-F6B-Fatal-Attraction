捕蝇草的四步捕猎法

1

分泌花蜜 · 引诱猎物

捕蝇草会分泌香甜的花蜜，散发出诱人的气味，吸引苍蝇、蚊子等小昆虫前来觅食。

2

触碰绒毛 · 产生信号

昆虫吸食花蜜时，会触碰叶片内侧的细小绒毛，触碰一次会产生电信号，但不会闭合。

3

两次触碰 · 叶片闭合

20秒内昆虫再次触碰绒毛，叶片会迅速闭合，边缘的长齿锁住猎物，让它无法逃脱。

4

分泌汁液 · 消化猎物

叶片闭合后分泌消化液，大约10天完成消化，吸收营养后，叶片重新打开等待下一个猎物。

文章精读 · 逐段拆解

段落A：捕猎的全过程 +

A fly smells nectar and lands on a green leaf. It starts to drink the sweet liquid. Suddenly, the fly's world turns green. The two sides of the leaf close against each other. Long green teeth lock together around it. This is not a nightmare. This is the work of the Venus flytrap, a carnivorous plant.

The Venus flytrap has a special way to catch its food. When an insect touches the tiny hairs on its leaves twice within 20 seconds, the leaf snaps shut. The insect can't get out. Then the plant produces a different liquid to digest the insect. It takes about 10 days for the plant to finish digesting. After that, the leaf opens again, waiting for the next meal.

段落B-C：科学家的新发现 +

For a long time, scientists knew the Venus flytrap could move, but they didn't really understand how it worked. Recently, a plant scientist named Alexander Volkov found out the secret.

When an insect touches the hairs on the leaf, it creates an electrical signal. The first touch makes a small signal. The second touch makes a bigger signal. This signal tells the leaf to close. This allows the plant to tell the difference between a drop of water and a moving creature. It doesn't want to waste energy closing for nothing!

段落D：为什么捕蝇草要吃虫？ +

The Venus flytrap is famous around the world. It grows in only a small area in North America. The soil there is poor and lacks important nutrients like nitrogen. So the plant has to catch insects to get the nutrients it needs to grow. It's a clever way to survive in a difficult environment.

知识点总结 · 巩固记忆

  • 捕蝇草是一种食虫植物，依靠捕猎昆虫获取生长所需的营养。
  • 捕蝇草捕猎分四步：分泌花蜜→触碰绒毛→叶片闭合→消化猎物。
  • 只有20秒内两次触碰绒毛，捕蝇草才会闭合，这是为了节约能量。
  • 捕蝇草通过电信号判断猎物，能区分无生命物体和活的昆虫。
  • 捕蝇草生长在营养贫瘠的土壤中，吃虫是它适应环境的聪明方式。
